About me

Mr. Yin (Member, IEEE) was born in Hunan Province, China, in 1996. He received the B.Eng. in information engineering and M.Eng. degrees in information and communication engineering from South China University of Technology, Guangzhou, China, in 2019 and 2022, respectively. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Electronics and ICT Engineering Technology, with The Faculty of Engineering and Architecture, Department of Information Technology (INTEC), WAVES-WICA, at Ghent University.

I enjoy playing the guitar, and I like badminton in terms of sports. Apart from that, in my spare time, I would like to write something which you can find here, just for record and sharing.
